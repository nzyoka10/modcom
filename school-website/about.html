About School

School Mission

Our mission is to provide quality education that fosters growth, academic excellence, and holistic development. We are committed to shaping responsible, well-rounded individuals who are ready to face future challenges.

Founded in 1924, our school has a long tradition of educational excellence. Our approach combines modern teaching methods with a strong foundation in values and character building, preparing students for a global future. Some of our major achievements include:

  • Accreditation by leading international education bodies
  • Consistently ranking among the top schools in Nairobi
  • Partnerships with global educational institutions

School Vision

Our vision is to cultivate a nurturing environment where students can explore their potential, develop critical thinking, and grow into compassionate, informed leaders. We envision a future where every student is equipped with the knowledge and skills to make a meaningful impact in their communities and beyond.

We believe that education goes beyond academics. Our school fosters creativity, innovation, and leadership through extracurricular activities, mentorship programs, and a vibrant school culture that encourages curiosity and a love of learning.

Community Engagement

We believe in the power of community and giving back. Our school has an active community service program where students participate in outreach projects, environmental sustainability initiatives, and partnerships with local organizations. This helps students develop a sense of responsibility and empathy toward the world around them.

Our Facilities

We are proud to offer state-of-the-art facilities that support both academic and extracurricular activities. Our campus features modern classrooms equipped with the latest technology, a well-stocked library, science and computer labs, sports grounds, and performing arts spaces.

We strive to create an environment where students feel comfortable and motivated to explore their interests, whether in science, sports, arts, or technology.

Our Curriculum

Our school offers a balanced curriculum designed to meet the needs of diverse learners. We emphasize critical thinking, problem-solving, and creative expression across all subjects. In addition to core subjects, students have access to a range of electives that allow them to pursue their passions in areas like art, music, languages, and technology.

We also offer a robust extracurricular program that includes sports, clubs, and leadership opportunities, helping students to develop their talents outside the classroom.
