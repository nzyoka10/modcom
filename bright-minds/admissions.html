Admissions

How to Apply

Join Bright Minds School and give your child the best start in life.

To apply for admission, follow these steps:

  1. Download the application form below or complete the online application.
  2. Submit the required documents either through the online form or in person at our admissions office.
  3. Once your application has been reviewed, we will schedule an assessment and an interview for your child.
  4. If successful, you will receive an official offer letter for your child's enrollment.
