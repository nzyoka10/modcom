Liver and Kale Rice Skillet with Lemon-Wine Sauce

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup rice (any type you prefer)
  • 1 lb liver (beef, chicken, or pork, cleaned and sliced)
  • 1 lemon (zested and juiced)
  • 2 cups kale (chopped)
  • 1 cup white wine (or red if you prefer)
  • 1-2 tbsp olive oil or butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Cook the Rice: Rinse the rice under cold water. In a medium saucepan, cook the rice according to the package instructions. Set aside once done.
  2. Prepare the Liver: Season the liver slices with salt and pepper. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat and add 1 tablespoon of olive oil or butter. Cook the liver for 2-3 minutes on each side, or until browned and cooked through. Remove from the skillet and set aside.
  3. Sauté the Kale: In the same skillet, add a little more oil or butter if needed. Add the chopped kale and sauté until wilted and tender, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Make the Lemon-Wine Sauce: Pour the white wine into the skillet with the kale. Bring to a simmer and let it reduce slightly, about 2 minutes. Add the lemon zest and juice. Stir to combine and let the sauce cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  5. Combine Everything: Slice the cooked liver into bite-sized pieces and return it to the skillet. Add the cooked rice and stir everything together, letting the flavors meld for a few minutes. Adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  6. Serve: Serve the liver and kale rice skillet hot, garnished with additional lemon zest if desired.
Note: This dish combines the rich flavors of the liver with the brightness of lemon and the earthy greens of kale, all balanced by the depth of the wine sauce. Enjoy your culinary creation!
