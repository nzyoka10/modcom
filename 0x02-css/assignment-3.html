About the Safari Rally

The Safari Rally is one of the toughest motorsport events in the world known for its rugged terrain and unpredictable weather conditions.

Rally Highlights

  • Challenging off-road courses
  • Iconic wildlife views
  • International competitors

Why Kenya?

Kenya's landscapes, from the savannahs to the mountains, provide the perfect backdrop for this epic rally. The Safari Rally brings together adventure, nature, and motorsport in a unique blend.
