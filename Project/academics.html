Academics

Explore our diverse curriculum and academic programs designed to foster learning and growth.

Curriculum

Competency-Based Curriculum (CBC)

The CBC emphasizes skills and competencies rather than traditional rote learning. It integrates critical thinking, creativity, and practical skills, preparing students for real-world challenges. The curriculum focuses on holistic development, including cognitive, social, and emotional growth.

8-4-4 System

The 8-4-4 system divides education into 8 years of primary school, 4 years of secondary school, and 4 years of university education. It aims to provide a broad-based education with a focus on academic subjects and general knowledge, building a strong foundation for further education and professional growth.

Programs

Academic Programs

We offer specialized programs in various subjects, including science, mathematics, languages, and social studies. Our programs are designed to meet the needs of students at different levels, from early childhood through to advanced studies.

Extracurricular Activities

In addition to academics, we provide a variety of extracurricular activities such as sports, arts, and leadership programs. These activities help students develop new skills, explore their interests, and contribute to a vibrant school community.

Academic Resources

Library

Our library offers a wide range of books, journals, and digital resources to support student research and learning. It provides a quiet space for studying and accessing educational materials.

Laboratories

We have well-equipped science and computer laboratories that provide hands-on learning experiences. These facilities help students gain practical skills and apply theoretical knowledge.

Support Services

We offer various support services, including academic counseling, tutoring, and special education resources, to ensure that every student receives the assistance they need to thrive.
