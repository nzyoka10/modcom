Frequently Asked Questions

Our trains operate from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily.

Tickets can be booked through our website or at the railway station.

We offer various discounts and promotions throughout the year. Please check our website for the latest offers.

Each passenger is allowed to carry one piece of hand luggage and one checked bag. Additional baggage may incur extra charges.

Pets are allowed on board with prior approval. They must be kept in a carrier and you should inform us at the time of booking.
