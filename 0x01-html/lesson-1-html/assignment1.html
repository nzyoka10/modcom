Homeland

Kenya, in East Africa, is known for its stunning landscape and diverse Wildlife. From the Maasai Mara's Great Migration to Mombasa's pristine beaches. Kenya offers incredible experiences. The national parks are perfect for safari adventure and observing Wildlife up close.

Nairobi

Nairobi, the capital city, blends modernity with tradition. Key attractions inlude Nairobi National Park, the Giraffe center, and the National Museum. It's a city with bustling markets and vibrant nightlife.

Mombasa

Mombasa is famous for it's beautiful beaches and historical sites. Fort Jesus and the lively Sawhili culture make it a unique destination. Enjoy activities like snorkeling and exploring the old town.

Kisumu

Kisumu, by Lake Victoria, is known for it's fishing industry and culture heritage. The lakeside views and local markets offer a different perspective of Kenya, making it a fascinating stop.
